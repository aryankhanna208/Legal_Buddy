A 22-year-old woman from Noida, who, along with her 23-year-old fiance, visited the Sai Upvan city forest in Ghaziabad, reportedly faced sexual harassment and extortion at the hands of three police officers on duty in a police response vehicle (PRV) on September 16. Following the incident, the suspects allegedly continued their harassment through phone calls and even visited the woman's residence. An FIR was filed based on the woman's complaint, and the three officers were charged under various sections of the IPC, including 354a (1)(ii), 323, 504, and 342, along with provisions of the Prevention of Corruption Act, at the Kotwali police station on September 28.
