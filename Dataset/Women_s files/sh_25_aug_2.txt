A harrowing episode transpired at Sai Upvan city forest in Ghaziabad on September 16, where a 22-year-old woman and her fiance faced the reprehensible actions of two purported law enforcement officers and an accomplice in civilian clothing. The trio, utilizing a Ghaziabad Police response vehicle, subjected the couple to prolonged sexual harassment and demanded an unjustifiable sum of Rs 10,000. The victim, in her complaint filed on September 28 at the Kotwali Nagar police station, detailed disturbing encounters, including physical assault and unwarranted visits to her residence by the accused.
