Man, son held for sexual harassment
Ludhiana: A 20-year-old woman accused her father and brother of sexually harassing her. The police lodged an FIR after getting a complaint in the matter and later arrested the accused. 
Inspector Hemant Malhotra said that the woman in her complaint said that her father was jobless and stayed at home. She alleged that her father and her younger brother had been sexually harassing her for long.
She claimed that the accused assaulted her mother too when she tried to stop them.
