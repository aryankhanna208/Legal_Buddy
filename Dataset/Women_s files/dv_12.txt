30-year-old Rhea Sharma, who, tragically, succumbed to suicide within a year of her marriage due to alleged dowry-related harassment. A court in Ambala has recently sentenced her 32-year-old husband, Arjun Singh, to life imprisonment in connection with the dowry death.
Rhea's family had accused Arjun and his family of subjecting her to relentless torment for dowry. The incident occurred on June 8, 2020, when Rhea was found hanging in her marital home.
