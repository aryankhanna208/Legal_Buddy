Mangaluru: Woman files dowry harassment case against husband, in-laws
Mangaluru, Nov 18: Keerthi Pai, a resident of KPT Vyasanagara in the city has filed a complaint in sixth JMFC court of the city, in which she has accused her husband and in-laws of harassing her for dowry.

A case is registered in this connection against natives of Kasargod, now living in apartment at Vyasanagar, Chethan V Bhakta, husband of Keerthi, his sister, mother and father. The marriage took place on December 18, 2022.
