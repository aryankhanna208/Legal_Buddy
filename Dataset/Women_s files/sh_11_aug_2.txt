The Nabarangpur community is reeling from shock and anger following the arrest of two teachers from a government-run residential school for the alleged gang rape of an 11-year-old Class 5 student. The survivor's father's brave decision to file a complaint has shed light on the distressing incident that transpired on Tuesday within the school premises.

The father detailed in his complaint that the accused teachers exploited the trust placed in them, luring his daughter to the school's toilet during regular school hours. The revelation has ignited a chorus of calls for justice from local residents and advocacy groups.

Community leaders and concerned citizens are demanding a thorough and expeditious investigation to ensure that the perpetrators are held accountable for their actions. The incident has sparked conversations about the need for heightened vigilance and stringent child protection measures across educational institutions.
