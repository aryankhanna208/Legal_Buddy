Sunita Malik filed a complaint against her husband, Inder Raj Malik, and in-laws, alleging mistreatment, starvation, and abuse, particularly during festivals, aimed at extracting money and possessions. Sunita endured severe physical and mental abuse without medical intervention. Her family's land was sold under duress to meet unlawful demands, with threats of harm and abduction if she didn't comply. The complaint highlights a pattern of coercion, harassment, and physical torture by her husband and in-laws.
