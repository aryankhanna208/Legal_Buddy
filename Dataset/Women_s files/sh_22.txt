Aluva child rape and murder: Kerala court says convict a paedophile, was accused in child abuse case in Delhi
A Kerala court on Tuesday noted that 28-year-old Ashfaq Alam, who was found guilty of the rape and murder of a 5-year-old child in Aluva, was accused of a similar crime in Delhi in 2018 but had absconded to Kerala as a result of which that case could not be taken to its conclusion
