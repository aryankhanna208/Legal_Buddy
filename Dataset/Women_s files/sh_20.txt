Mangaluru: Woman files dowry harassment case against husband, in-laws
 Keerthi Pai, a resident of KPT Vyasanagara in the city has filed a complaint in sixth JMFC court of the city, in which she has accused her husband and in-laws of harassing her for dowry.
In her complaint, Keerthi accused her husband and in-laws of asking her to bring more dowry and torturing her mentally and physically. A case has been filed regarding the same.
