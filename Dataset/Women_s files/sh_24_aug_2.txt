A harrowing incident unfolded in Shamli district, Uttar Pradesh, as a 16-year-old girl, driven to desperation by relentless pressure from her alleged sexual harasser, Talib, tragically ended her own life. The Jhinjhana police station, having previously dealt with this distressing matter, immediately swung into action upon receiving a fresh complaint from the girl's father. Talib now faces the serious charge of abetment to suicide," stated Additional Superintendent of Police O P Singh, emphasizing the severity of the crime and the swiftness of the police response.
