Haryana school sexual harassment case: Teacher facing sexual exploitation charge appointed as acting principal

JIND: Following the suspension of an accused principal for the alleged sexual harassment of 60 minor girls in a government girl senior secondary school in Haryana, controversy erupted again as the temporary charge of the school was handed over to a teacher was is facing prior sexual exploitation charges.
The teacher, previously accused four years ago, is now appointed as the acting principal.
Earlier, three teachers faced similar charges after an anonymous letter from girls to Prime Minister Narendra Modi. FIRs under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) were filed against them. Now, with 60 girls coming forward against the accused principal, serious allegations have emerged.
