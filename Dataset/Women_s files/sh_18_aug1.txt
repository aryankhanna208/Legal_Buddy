
"Heading home on my two-wheeler with a friend, we encountered a group of men on motorcycles tailing us. Although we could hear them calling out names, fear held us back from stopping or turning around. In an attempt to shake them off, I accelerated our two-wheeler and raced towards the main road, hoping to reach a more populated area before they could catch up. Thankfully, the men abandoned their pursuit once we reached the main road. Without a doubt, it was one of the most frightening rides we've ever had," she explained.
