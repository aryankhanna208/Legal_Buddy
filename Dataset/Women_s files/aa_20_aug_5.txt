Within the realm of the Supreme Court, a case laden with a dispute over encroachment took a dark turn as the accused resorted to pouring acid on the deceased. The aftermath saw the legal machinery invoking Section 302 of the IPC, leading to a stern and definitive sentence. The main accused, now entwined in the judicial fabric, bore the consequence of rigorous imprisonment for life, underscoring the gravity of the actions that resulted in the victim's demise.
