The victim, Lata, finds herself entangled in a distressing situation following her husband's demise. Deprived of her matrimonial home, she and her child face an uncertain future with no means of support. Despite the emotional and financial challenges, Lata contends that existing legal frameworks do not mandate her brother-in-law to provide maintenance. She seeks justice and reinstatement of her rights to the joint family property, emphasizing the emotional and economic hardships she endures. The victim implores the court to consider the broader implications of her predicament on her child's welfare and her own well-being.
