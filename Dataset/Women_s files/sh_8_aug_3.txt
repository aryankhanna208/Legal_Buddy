Allegedly, a 22-year-old woman and her 23-year-old fiance, visiting the Sai Upvan city forest in Ghaziabad, fell victim to sexual harassment and extortion by three police officers on duty in a police response vehicle (PRV) on September 16. The harassment reportedly continued with phone calls and a visit to the woman's residence by the suspects. Responding to the woman's complaint, an FIR was filed, and the three officers were charged under IPC sections 354a (1)(ii), 323, 504, and 342, in addition to provisions of the Prevention of Corruption Act, at Kotwali police station on September 28.
