In a legal chessboard maneuver witnessed in a Madras High Court case, a nurse and a compounder strategically countered accusations of conspiracy. Their alleged vendetta, manifesting as a corrosive assault on a doctor, purportedly avenged an alleged rape by the doctor against the nurse. The legal battleground featured charges under IPC Sections 302 and 109, yet the accused emerged unscathed, skillfully navigating through the lack of evidentiary support.
