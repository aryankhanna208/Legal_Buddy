The initiation of legal proceedings in this case was precipitated by a police report filed by the Appellant, Sandhya, against her husband for alleged assault. The report served as a critical document that prompted the legal intervention, shedding light on the distressing conditions within her matrimonial home. The police, as the first responders, played a pivotal role in documenting the victim's complaint, a foundational step that set the legal machinery in motion. Sandhya's courage in reporting the assault reflects the importance of law enforcement in addressing domestic violence, underlining the crucial role of police in ensuring the safety and rights of victims. This police report became the catalyst for subsequent legal actions, emphasizing the integral role of law enforcement in the pursuit of justice in cases of marital discord and violence.
