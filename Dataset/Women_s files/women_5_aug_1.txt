ncident Report - Dowry Harassment Case

Rajesh Sharma (Appellant) and Sneha Sharma (Respondent) wed on November 28th, 2012. Despite the dowry given by Sneha's father, the Appellants allegedly harassed her, demanding Rs. 3,00,000/- and a vehicle. Following a terminated pregnancy, the Appellant abandoned Sneha. A lawsuit ensued, with the wife claiming dowry harassment and pregnancy torment. High Court denied the Appellants' request to cancel summons, prompting an appeal to the Supreme Court. Appellants contend no intent for dowry demands.
