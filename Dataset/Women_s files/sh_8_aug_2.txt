During a visit to the Sai Upvan city forest in Ghaziabad with her 23-year-old fiance, a 22-year-old woman from Noida was reportedly sexually harassed and extorted by three police officers on duty in a police response vehicle (PRV) on September 16. Subsequently, the suspects allegedly continued their harassment by making phone calls and even paying a visit to the woman's residence. In response to the woman's complaint, an FIR was lodged, and the three officers were charged under IPC sections 354a (1)(ii), 323, 504, and 342, along with provisions of the Prevention of Corruption Act, at Kotwali police station on September 28.
